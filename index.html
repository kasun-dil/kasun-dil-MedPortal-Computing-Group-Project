Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is MedPortal?

MedPortal is an online platform designed to provide counseling services and mental health support. We connect individuals with professional counselors for secure, confidential, and convenient therapy sessions.

2. Are my counseling sessions confidential?

Yes! We prioritize privacy and security. All sessions are fully confidential and encrypted to protect your personal information.

3. How do I know if I need counseling?

If you are feeling overwhelmed, stressed, anxious, or struggling with personal, emotional, or relationship issues, counseling can help you gain clarity, support, and effective coping strategies.

4. What happens during a counseling session?

The duration of counseling varies from person to person. Some people see improvements within a few sessions, while others may need long-term therapy based on their concerns and progress.

5. Can counseling help with anxiety and depression?

Yes, professional counseling provides tools and techniques to manage anxiety, depression, and other mental health challenges effectively.

6. What should I do if counseling doesn’t seem to be helping?

Discuss your concerns with your counselor. They may adjust their approach or suggest a different therapy method to better suit your needs.

7. Can I choose my counselor?

Yes! You can browse counselor profiles, read about their expertise, and select the one that best fits your needs.
